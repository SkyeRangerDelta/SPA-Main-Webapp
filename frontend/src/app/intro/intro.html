Fostering Economy, Infrastructure, and Fun

The official office of the Sarim Port Authority: the governing entity of the CCG Minecraft server. Resources and tools for building a thriving economy, enhancing infrastructure, and creating engaging experiences. The office wrests plenty of records from the city archives, including cartography, trade routes, infrastructure plans, shipping manifests, and more. The office also manages the world economy, including trade agreements, tariffs, and economic policies.

The Sarim Port Authority is dedicated to fostering a vibrant economy, enhancing infrastructure, and ensuring a fun and engaging experience for all players. Whether you're a builder, trader, or adventurer, the Port Authority provides the tools and resources you need to thrive in the CCG Minecraft server. Staff are available to assist with queries, suggestions, any gameplay issues.
