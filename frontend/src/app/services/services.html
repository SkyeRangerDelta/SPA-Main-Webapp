Harbour Services

For the Benefit of Masters, Merchants, and Mariners

All ports under the Seal of the Sarim Port Authority are required to maintain a standard complement of services for the safety, commerce, and welfare of those who sail under its watch. These provisions are available to all vessels lawfully entering the harbour, subject to local tariffs and SPA regulation.

Medical & Welfare

Sick Bay & Hospital

Staffed by surgeons offering treatment for injuries, ailments, and illnesses.

Quarantine Section

Separate berth and inspection area for vessels or persons suspected of carrying contagious diseases. Masters are to comply with all quarantine orders without delay.

Relief Stores

Reserved rations, clean water, blankets, and basic provisions for citizens and mariners in distress. Supplies may be subject to policy and availability based on situation and local conditions.

Placement & Employment Office

Registry of available seafarers, dockworkers, and other maritime personnel. Masters seeking crew or employers seeking workers may register here. The SPA does not guarantee employment but facilitates introductions.

Administration & Registry

Port Registry Office

The local main SPA office for the registration of vessels, cargo manifests, and crew rosters. Any and all documentation, maritime reports, admiralty notices, and other official records must be filed here. This office also handles reports of misconduct, accidents, and other incidents involving vessels, personnel, port operations, and any municipal disturbance.

Masters, merchants, and others with SPA business may find issuance and verification of any official documents, licenses, or permits. Letters or marque and correspondence may be posted here or request sealing.

Harbour Dues & Customs Office

Collection of harbour dues, tariffs, and customs duties. Receipts issued under SPA seal.

Navigation & Safety

Harbour Pilots

Skilled navigators available to assist in the safe entry and departure of vessels.

Tide & Weather Board

Updated daily with tide tables, wind conditions, and storm warnings.

Beacon, Signal, and Lighthouse Maintenance

Ports are equipped with various navigational and signal aids updated and maintained by the SPA. Availability varies by port.

Cargo & Trade

Bonded Warehouses

Secure storage for goods in SPA custody, subject to customs inspection and duties. Onward passage may be arranged.

Inspection and Weight Services

Warehouses and cargo areas are equipped with facilities for the inspection, weighing, and measuring of goods for legal verification.

Hazardous Goods Handling

Isolated compounds and transport for volatile or dangerous cargoes including redstone, gunpowder, blaze powder, and other hazardous materials.

Ship Chandler & Requisitions Office

Suppliers for provisions, equipment, and repairs. Masters may requisition necessary items for their vessels, subject to availability and local tariffs. The SPA does not guarantee supply but will assist in finding local merchants or suppliers.

Security & Enforcement

Harbour Guard

All SPA ports are patrolled by some level of harbour guard or watch. Some ports may be equipped with a barracks or station for the guard. The guard is charged with maintaining order, providing defense, enforcing SPA regulations, and assisting mariners in distress.

Jail & Confinement Cells

Internment holdings are available for the detention of persons accused of crimes, violations, or other misconduct.

Courts & Hearing Halls

Some ports may provide facilities for the adjudication of maritime disputes, criminal cases, and other legal matters.
