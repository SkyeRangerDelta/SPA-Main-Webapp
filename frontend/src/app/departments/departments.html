Departments

The Sarim Port Authority maintains a series of sub-offices and departments that ensure smoother and more efficient operations. Each department has its own unique responsibilities and functions, contributing to the overall success of the port.

Office of the Harbour Registry

The Office of the Harbour Registry is responsible for maintaining any and all records of port and government activities. This includes ship registrations, cargo manifests, administrative policy, charters, and official correspondence. The office ensures that all documentation is accurate and up-to-date, facilitating smooth operations and compliance with regulations.

Office of Cartographic Records

The Office of Cartographic Records manages all maps, charts, and navigational aids across the known world. This department is responsible for updating and distributing these materials to ensure safe and efficient navigation for all vessels.
