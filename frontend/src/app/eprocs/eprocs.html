Emergency Procedures

In an Emergency Now?

STOP! If you are in immediate danger, please notify your local emergency services immediately. The local SPA OEM will be made aware of the situation and will respond as necessary.

Concerning Emergencies

It is only natural that emergencies will arise from time to time. The Sarim Port Authority takes matters such as these with great sincerity and urgency. A safer harbour is a better harbour for all. The following document outlines simple procedures to follow in the event of an emergency. All emergencies are handled case-by-case via the SPA Office of Emergency Management. It should be imperative that you are familiar with the local SPA branch at each port you patron.
