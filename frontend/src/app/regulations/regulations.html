Rules and Regulations of the Clan

The Sarim Port Authority (SPA) upholds a series of regulatory statutes that govern both the operations of the authority and the activities and policies of its subject municipalities, citizens, and businesses. As the SPA is representative of CCG; the rules applied by CCG are applicable here.

CC Gaming Rules

Rule Avoidance is not Tolerated

  • Follow all General Conduct Rules
  • Be respectful of others. If someone does not like your behavior, stop or go to a different channel/area.
  • Please only stream or record in a channel or area after obtaining permission from the others around to do so.

General Conduct Rules

  • Be respectful to others, their personhood, beliefs, race, gender, nationality, disability, or any other way they may differ from you. (Matthews 7:12)
  • Obey all laws and end user agreements. (No sharing or discussing pirated content whatsoever.) ( Romans 13:8 )
  • If you feel someone to be guilty of any wrong doing, please talk to them privately or not at all. (Matthew 18:15)
  • Please refrain fromm advertising or recruiting for anything anywhere without prior approval.

Minecraft Server Rules

  • Follow all CCG Rules
  • No griefing.
  • No stealing.
  • No killing/trapping players without their consent.
  • Prior to building, ensure you do not encroach on neighboring build plans.
  • No cheating; including duplication exploits, x-ray packs and glitches, or utilizing unfair advantage mods.
This excludes visual fidelity and client QoL mods such as shaders, resources packs, etc.

SPA Code of Regulations (abridged)

  • Entry & Mooring:

    Heave-to outside the harbour until cleared by the harbour guard. Berth only at posts assigned by the harbourmaster.

  • Pilotage:

    Where pilots are posted, no foreign master may enter or depart without one aboard.

  • Customs & Manifests:

    Declare cargo, crew, and passengers on arrival. False ledgers invite seizure and fines.

  • Hazardous Stores:

    Gunpowder/TNT, blaze powder, redstone dust, and lava buckets mmust be lodged in the hazard yard or sealed under SPA order. No open flame on the quay.

  • Quarantine:

    Hoist yellow if illness is aboard. No landing or disembarking until examined and cleared by the harbour surgeon.

  • Conduct & Security:

    Brawling, theft, greifing, or sabotage earns brig time or ban. Weapons sheathed within the bounds of the port.

  • Signals & Movement:

    Observe flags and bell codes. No ramming, channel blocking, or elytra flight around the docks.

  • Cargo Handling:

    Hoists and cranes only at marked bays. Drop-chutes and TNT-powered machinery or devices are forbidden within the port. City/town licensure may be required within the city bounds.

  • Redstone Devices:

    Clocks, alarms, and contraptions of any redstone nature must be toggleable and non-lagging. The harbourmaster reserves the right to inspect and disable any device deemed disturbing or hazardous.

  • Fires & Lights:

    Braziers and lanterns only; no naked flame near powder stores, magazines, warehouses, or ships taking on stores.

  • Fishing & Resource Harvesting:

    Nets and tridents are barred within the port and dock markers. Do not strip seabed blocks or harbour walls.

  • Wrecks & Salvaging:

    Report all groundings or derelicts. Salvage operations are directed by the SPA with finders law consideration.

  • Mutiny & Disorder:

    Masters report insubordination. SPA has the right to board and restore order.

  • Curfew:

    After the night bell, vessels require permission from the harbourmaster to depart. No entry or exit without clearance.

  • Tribunals & Fines:

    Disputes are heard at the nearest arbitration or hearing hall. Fines are payable in diamonds, or ingots of equal value at the customs office.

For the complete code and penalty structure, see SPA Code of Regulations available at all SPA registry office.
